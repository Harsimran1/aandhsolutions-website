## How to choose the right AI implementation partner

Before signing, ask these questions:

1. What production AI systems have you shipped that are still in use?
2. How do you handle AI quality control, fallback logic, and human-in-the-loop review?
3. Which existing systems (CRM, docs, comms, ops tools) will be integrated in phase one?
4. How do you measure impact in the first 60-90 days?
5. What is your post-launch monitoring and iteration model?
