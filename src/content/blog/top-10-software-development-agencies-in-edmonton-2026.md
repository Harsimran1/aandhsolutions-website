## How to choose the right Edmonton agency

Before signing any proposal, ask these 5 questions:

1. Who exactly will be on my team, and what have they shipped recently?
2. How do you define scope and handle change requests?
3. What cadence do we use for demos, QA, and release approvals?
4. What does post-launch support include in writing?
5. What outcomes do you optimize for in the first 90 days?

If an agency cannot answer these clearly, keep interviewing.
