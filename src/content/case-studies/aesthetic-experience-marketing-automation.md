## Project Overview

The Aesthetic Experience is a beauty company in West Linn, Oregon offering personalized facial treatments, wellness services, and at-home beauty products. Their team manages appointments through Boulevard, tracks leads in HubSpot, and sells products through a Shopify store — but those systems weren't talking to each other.

We were brought in to automate their sales and marketing operations end to end: syncing customer data across platforms, eliminating repetitive manual tasks, and building the infrastructure for scalable marketing campaigns.

## The Challenge

The team was spending significant time every week on work that should have been automated:

- Manually booking virtual appointments and sending confirmation emails (5+ hours/week)
- Copying lead and contact data between Boulevard, HubSpot, and Shopify by hand (20+ hours/week)
- Manually setting up marketing campaigns from scratch each time with no reusable template
- No Google Calendar integration for virtual bookings — appointments existed in Boulevard but weren't surfaced to the team's calendars

With a high-priority Mother's Day campaign on the horizon, the window to get everything running was tight.

## Our Solution

We implemented four interconnected automation streams:

**1. Multi-platform data sync**
We connected Boulevard (appointment software), HubSpot (CRM), and Shopify (e-commerce) so that customer and lead data flows automatically across all three. New contacts in Boulevard sync to HubSpot; product and inventory data syncs between Shopify and Boulevard. The team no longer manually reconciles records between platforms.

**2. Appointment and calendar automation**
Virtual appointment bookings in Boulevard now automatically create Google Calendar events, with custom text sent to clients. This eliminated the manual appointment creation and follow-up email workflow entirely.

**3. Custom Shopify post-purchase app**
We built a custom Shopify app that collects additional contact information after a customer completes a purchase. This gives the team richer lead data in HubSpot and improves targeting for future campaigns.

**4. Reusable marketing campaign template**
We built a base campaign process in Shopify that the team can replicate for future promotions. The Mother's Day campaign — bundling treatments and products — was the first deployment. It launched on time despite a tight deadline that required overtime to hit.

## Outcome

The automation work immediately freed up significant time for the team and produced measurable revenue results:

- **5 hours/week** reclaimed from appointment creation and client email workflows
- **20 hours/week** eliminated from manual lead syncing and campaign setup
- **~$1,000 in campaign revenue** generated from the inaugural Mother's Day campaign
- Platforms now stay in sync automatically — no manual reconciliation needed

The client noted that A & H Solutions worked overtime to ensure the Mother's Day campaign launched on time, and that the team genuinely cared about the outcome: no money was lost due to delays.
