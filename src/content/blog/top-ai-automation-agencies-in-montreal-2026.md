If you are evaluating AI automation partners in Montreal, this list is written for buyers who need production-grade implementation — not a discovery workshop followed by a slide deck.

Montreal occupies a unique position in the Canadian AI landscape: it is home to Yoshua Bengio and the Mila Quebec AI Institute, one of the world's most influential AI research hubs. That research concentration shapes the local market — Montreal has deep AI talent, a multilingual (English/French) delivery culture, and strong representation in sectors like finance, logistics, media, life sciences, and enterprise software. The challenge for buyers is finding implementation partners who can translate research depth into operational results. The agencies on this list are evaluated on that basis.

3. Ability to ship systems that connect real tools: CRMs, ERPs, communication platforms, and custom APIs.

## How to evaluate an AI automation partner

Before committing, these questions separate real implementers from strategy-only firms:

1. Can you show a live automation system you shipped that is still running in production?
2. What happens when an automation step fails — how does the system recover, and how do you find out?
3. Which specific tools in our stack have you integrated before?
4. How do you scope and price a project — fixed scope, time-and-materials, or retainer?
5. What does handoff look like — do we get documentation, or are we dependent on you to make changes?
